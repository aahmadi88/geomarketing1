Why Geomarketing


In marketing, Geomarketing is a interdisciplinary approach that uses geolocation (geographic information) in the process of planning and implementation of marketing activities. It can be used in any aspect of the marketing: the product, price, promotion, or place (geo targeting). Market segments can also correlate with location, and this can be useful in targeted marketing.

This technology allows you to analyze your data with a variety of maps and visualize your company data on different scales.

Most outputs are digital maps that allow the market analysis team and business owners to analyze and view their market from a different platform and perspective.

A map says more than 1,000 words.

Market Research Industry


Today one of the most important factors in the success and failures of your strategies in marketing and economic activities, is level of quality and access to data.

Geomarketing models and software can play a decisive role in many market research activities specially in data quality using models of geographic and non-geographical factors simultaneously:

  • Volume and market share of different brands and products in different places.

  • Estimating the level of competition, sampling in different areas.

  • Local and regional Geomarketing maps, billboard location analysis and environmental advertisements.

  • Segmentation of sale areas and consumer of goods and services.

  • Display data and information in the form of digital maps.

Basic Model Structure


  • First, it is necessary to geocode all types of data

  • After initial preprocessing, base maps are extracted

  • Base maps are combined and analyzed

  • And after processing and correcting again, the desired layers are selected

  • The selected layers are recombined and modified to create the model

  • After building the models, their output can be exported as maps and charts
